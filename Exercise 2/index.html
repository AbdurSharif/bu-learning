Spring Harmonies

Spring Harmonies is one of the semi-annual concerts that take place at Flato Theatre. This concert showcases the pieces and hard work of each ensemble, as they perform their pieces for the audience.

The ensembles that will be performing are:

  • Grade 12 Wind Symphony
  • Grade 11 Wind Symphony
  • Grade 10 Concert Band
  • Grade 9 Concert Band
  • String Orchestra
  • Jazz Band
  • Solo ensemble performances
